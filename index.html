Who We Are

GS – Gioventù Studentesca

GS (Student Youth) is a group of high school students and some adults who want to discover the reasonableness and beauty of the Christian faith in every part of life: study, friendship, leisure, current events, and prayer.

CL – Communion and Liberation

Communion and Liberation is a Catholic movement that began in Italy in the 1950s from the experience of Fr. Luigi Giussani. Today it is present in many countries around the world and proposes friendship, education, and a journey of faith lived in daily life.

Who We Support

AVSI Foundation

AVSI is an international non-profit organization, born in Italy in 1972. It carries out development and humanitarian projects in many countries, with special attention to education, work, and the dignity of each person.

The Tents Campaign

The Tents Campaign is a series of events organized all over the world to raise funds and awareness for AVSI projects. By joining this Gala, we support concrete projects that bring hope to children, young people, families, and communities.

You can learn more on the official AVSI Tents Campaign page: AVSI – Tents Campaign .
